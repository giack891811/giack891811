testable and subject to human approval.

platform behind the SYNTROPIIX product vision. It combines employee agents,
risk-graded permissions, human-in-the-loop execution, hybrid local/cloud model
routing and audit-oriented controls for regulated European organizations.

My source repositories remain private, while architecture, engineering
decisions, validation evidence and project summaries are documented in my
public portfolio.

Before moving into AI engineering, I spent more than 15 years working with
security, electronic access-control and electromechanical systems. That
background shaped how I build software: diagnose carefully, document clearly,
respect safety boundaries and design for real operating conditions.
